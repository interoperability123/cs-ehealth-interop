# Solution Validator Instructions

As input, you have got a text called challenge articulation, a text called solution articulation, and a paper (in the form of a PDF file) called solution source.

## Task
 
The paper may contain discussions on one or several challenges as well as one or several solutions. 
Identify the challenge and solution discussed in the paper that correspond to the challenge in the challenge articulation and the solution in the solution articulation. 
Analyse critically to what extent the solution articulation is true to the discussion of the corresponding solution.
